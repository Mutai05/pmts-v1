FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

The Projects Monitoring and Tracking System (PMTS) is a digital platform designed by the County Government of Trans-Nzoia to improve the management, monitoring, and evaluation of county projects. It provides real-time updates on project statuses, budgets, and timelines, fostering transparency and accountability in project implementation.

PMTS promotes transparency by making project data publicly accessible in real-time. Citizens and stakeholders can track progress, review budgets, and access reports, ensuring accountability among project implementers and fostering public trust.

PMTS offers detailed information on all county projects, including descriptions, current statuses (e.g., completed, ongoing), timelines, budgets, progress reports, and supporting documents. It also lists involved stakeholders and their roles.

Yes, PMTS includes a feedback feature allowing users to submit comments or report issues directly through the platform. This helps the county address concerns promptly and improve system functionality.

PMTS is accessible to the public, county officials, and project stakeholders. The public dashboard is open to all, while certain administrative features are restricted to authorized personnel via login credentials.
